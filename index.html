Welcome to UGA's ACM Chapter

The ACM Chapter at UGA is a student run organization dedicated to fostering the growth of the technology community at UGA. We host events that give students the chance to socialize and connect with other like-minded students in the community. These events will often include food, beverages, and some sort of gift bag to spur group morale. At ACM, we also host workshops and research panels that help students jumpstart and prepare for their professional careers.
